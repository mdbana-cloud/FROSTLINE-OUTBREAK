❄️ FROSTLINE OUTBREAK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This privacy policy applies to the game Frostline Outbreak (developed by AURORA CORE / Linebreakers Team) and its use of Epic Online Services (EOS).

1. Data collected via EOS

When you play Frostline Outbreak using Epic Online Services, the following information may be processed:
- Epic account ID and display name
- In-game progress, stats, loadouts
- Session information (match history, performance)
- Friend lists and party invitations

2. How we use data

Data is used exclusively for:
✔ Multiplayer matchmaking and hosting
✔ Cross-platform saves and progression
✔ Anti-cheat and fair play enforcement
✔ Leaderboards and achievements

3. Third-party services

Our game uses Epic Online Services SDK (by Epic Games, Inc.). Their privacy policy applies to account management and online interactions: Epic Games Privacy Policy.

4. Your rights

You may request deletion of your game data by contacting privacy@frostline-outbreak.com (under development). Data tied to your Epic account can be managed via your Epic account settings.

5. Children under 13

Frostline Outbreak is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to this policy

Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
